Competition Events

1. Classic Powerlifting Championship (Men & Women)

This category consists of three lifts:

  • Squat – The ultimate test of lower-body strength
  • Bench Press – A challenge for upper-body power
  • Deadlift – A true measure of full-body strength

Each lifter gets three attempts per lift. The highest successful attempt is recorded as their final score.

2. Open Deadlift Competition (Men Only)

This standalone event is exclusively for the deadlift. It tests participants' ability to lift the heaviest possible weight from the ground to a standing position. The best single deadlift attempt will determine the winner.
